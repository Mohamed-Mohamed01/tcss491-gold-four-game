How To Play
Goal
Follow the objective text at the top of the screen and progress through the levels. Collect keys to unlock exits in early stages, then face the arena challenge.
Controls
Move W A S D ↑ ← ↓ →
Attack 1 Space
Attack 2 (AoE) Y
Interact / Talk E
Menu P
Objectives & Hints
Watch the hint text at the top of the screen. It guides you through keys, arena progress, and special interactions.
Pickups
Coins help you buy upgrades. Hearts restore health when collected.
Shop
On the boss level, visit the shop to upgrade health, damage, and speed. Press E near the shop to open it.
NPCs
Talk to NPCs for guidance. Press E when prompted.
Arena & Boss
Enter the arena when ready. Each round grows tougher, and the boss returns stronger every time. Time your attacks and manage your health carefully.
Story Scrolls
Level 1 includes a story scroll that provides lore. Walk up to it to read.
Exits
Each level has an exit hidden somewhere on the map. Complete the required objectives first to unlock it. Once your tasks are finished, explore and find the exit to continue. On the boss level, progression leads into the arena encounter.
Tips
Keep moving, watch enemy patterns, and save health pickups for the hardest fights.
